Common Questions

How fast do you respond?

We aim to respond within 24 hours. For urgent farmer issues, WhatsApp response is usually within 1-2 hours.

Can I visit your office?

Yes! Email partnerships@bluefoodos.com to schedule a visit. We're based in Abeokuta, Ogun State. Tours available Mon-Thu, 10am-4pm.

Do you have a press kit?

Yes. Email media requests to hello@bluefoodos.com. We provide logos, fact sheets, founder bios, and sample imagery.

Are you hiring?

Yes! Check open roles on our Team page or email careers@bluefoodos.com.

Can we partner with you?

We're open to partnerships. Email partnerships@bluefoodos.com with details on your organization and what you'd like to explore.
