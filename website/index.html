Five gaps. One solution.

🔍 No Monitoring

Fish farmers work blind. No early warning when water quality crashes and fish die.

💰 No Financing

Cage kits cost ₦500K-₦2M upfront. Most farmers can't access credit without collateral.

🎒 No Feed Access

Feed is 50% of production costs. Small farmers buy retail at 2-3x wholesale prices.

❄️ No Cold Chain

30-50% of harvest spoils before reaching market. No refrigerated transport or storage.

🤝 No Buyers

Farmers have no direct market access. Middlemen take 40% margins. No price transparency.

Five problems. One subscription.

☀️ Solar Sensor Buoys

2-4 hour early warning before fish mortality. Monitor dissolved oxygen, pH, temperature real-time.

🏗️ HDPE Cage Kits

Financed through harvest deductions. No upfront payment. Built with local materials.
