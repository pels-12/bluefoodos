Nigeria's fish crisis has a platform.

853km of coastline. $1.5 billion in fish imports. 2 million smallholder farmers working in isolation. BlueFoodOS connects them.

The gap

Fish farmers cannot scale on guesswork.

Water quality changes fast. Feed eats profit. Cold storage is hard to find. Cages cost too much upfront. Banks need records before they lend.

BlueFoodOS connects the missing pieces. Farmers get clearer decisions. Buyers get better supply. Lenders get records they can trust.

One connected platform

Five tools working around the farmer.

01

Water intelligence

Solar sensor buoys track oxygen, pH, and temperature before problems become losses.

02

Cage financing

Farmers can access stronger cages and pay through harvest deductions.

03

Feed procurement

Farm clusters buy together, reduce feed costs, and see input quality more clearly.

04

Cold chain

Solar cold storage and transport help farmers keep more value after harvest.

05

Credit history

Farm activity, costs, water data, and harvest records become proof for lenders.
